What is Cornell Notes?

Cornell(please accept me) Notes is a very popular notetaking method used by many people
around the world in order to create easily comprehensible notes that are very effective
for those late-night, or just normal study sessions(I know that you have done them).
Cornell Notes consist of three sections: The Main Notes Section, where you take your notes,
the Cue section, where you can put crucial vocabulary terms or central ideas that are
related to your very important notes. Finally, there is the summary section, where
you summmarize everything that you have written down. In the end, this notetaking strategy
can be effective, proving to you that maybe you should change your notes up a bit
even if you believe that you are taking good notes right now, would you?
